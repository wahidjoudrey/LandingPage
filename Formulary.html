Da Vinci PDex Plan-Net Drug Formulary

An Overview of the Drug Formulary API using HL7 FHIR Resources

What is Drug Formulary?

The Da Vinci PDex Plan-Net Implementation Guide defines a FHIR-based standard for drug formulary data exchange between payers, third parties, and consumers. It helps meet the CMS Interoperability and Patient Access Rule by enabling a standardized, queryable drug formulary.

It supports the publication of drug formulary data including medications, their coverage details, and the corresponding coverage details.

Core FHIR Resources in Plan-Net

List

Represents a list of medications. It may be a top-level network or a sub-organization within a larger network.

Key Fields: resourceType,meta, id, language, active, name, type, telecom, address, contact
